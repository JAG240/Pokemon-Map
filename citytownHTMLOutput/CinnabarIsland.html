Cinnabar Island

A town used to be here until it was swept away by an eruption.The Pokemon Mansion is a decrepit, burned-down mansion on Cinnabar Island. It got its name because a famous Pokemon Researcher once lived there. The Pokemon Mansion has four different floors. Doors can be unlocked in the Mansion by pressing switches hidden in Mewtwo statues. There is a volcano on Cinnabar Island which gives it its name. The volcano is a prominent feature of the island, and is where the Cinnabar Gym is located, split into two sections. One is inside of the volcano, and has an arena suspended over the lava inside one of the chambers, while the other is atop the volcano's crater.The Cinnabar Gym is the official Gym of Cinnabar Island, it is based on Fire-type Pokemon and the Gym Leader is Blaine. Trainers who defeat him receive the Volcano Badge.
