Pewter City

A quiet city nestled between rugged mountains and rocks.A famous museum is located in the northwestern part of Pewter City and is one of the city's main attractions. The main building is open to the public for 50 and features several historical artifacts, including Aerodactyl and Kabutops Fossils, and a model of a space shuttle.The Pewter Gym is the official Gym of Pewter City. It is based on Rock-type Pokemon. The Gym Leader is Brock. Trainers that defeat him receive the Boulder Badge.
