Lavender Town

A small town covered in a beautiful hue of purple. Pokemon Tower is a seven-floor graveyard that holds the grave of departed Pokemon. On all seven floors, the tower houses hundreds of graves of deceased Pokemon. Many people visit the building daily to pay their respects to the fallen. On all floors other than the first two, though, the possibility of being attacked by wild Pokemon is present. Populated by Channelers, it was notable for numerous ghost sightings (Pokemon killed by Team Rocket), as well as the location of Team Rocket's kidnapping of Mr. Fuji. Although players can encounter Ghost-type Pokemon here, they can not be seen or captured unless they have a Silph Scope. The tower was converted to the Kanto Radio Tower, with all graves being moved to either the House of Memories or underground. The new House of Memories is placed where the Poke Mart has been before. The Kanto radio tower can be entered, like its Johto counterpart, but only the ground floor, as the upper floors are completely off limits. This was done to prevent a similar take over that was seen in Johto. Talking to the receptionist on the ground floor, the player can receive a Pokegear radio card for Kanto. The Lavender Volunteer Pokemon House located in central Lavender Town, was founded by Mr. Fuji in order to care for abandoned and orphaned Pokemon. Many members of the organization live in the safe house, looking after the abandoned Pokemon. Many Pokemon have stayed at the Volunteer Pokemon House over time, including Psyduck, Nidorino, and after the Team Rocket attack on the Pokemon Tower, Cubone.
