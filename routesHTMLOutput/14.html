Route 14

From its north end at the maze of Route 13, the path heads south along the east edge of a long treeline. After a distance of roughly 20 paces, there is a gap in the treeline, allowing Trainers who can use Cut or Chop Down to remove a small tree and to pass through. Just past this point is the first Trainer on the route.
